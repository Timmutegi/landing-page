Frequently Asked Questions

  • Bluewave Telemedicine uses a technological platform to allow patients via video call to see a Kenyan licensed medical practitioner online upon booking. You as a patient do not need to travel long distances to see a doctor.
  • Bluewave Telemedicine can provide you with a prescription or a diagnosis for your symptoms all online.
  • We can also provide specialist referrals and order tests.
  • Your medical summary and details remain on your portal and you can view them anytime upon logging in.
  • You enroll into the service by registering on the portal, paying the required fees and booking for consultation, a summary of the booking is sent to you via SMS. Once the booking is done, our practitioner accepts the booking and treatment is conducted via video call.

Bluewave Telemedicine is available to all Kenyans of all ages. Children below 18 years should be accompanied by a Parent/Guardian.

You can find out ALL the information on Bluewave Telemedicine by dialing the Bluewave contact center on 0739599914. You can also read more on Bluewave on our social media channels: Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, YouTube and LinkedIn.

When enrolling for Bluewave Telemedicine you are required to provide your First Name, Last Name, ID number and Date of Birth.

The medical consultation services to be offered via video call by physicians through the Bluewave Telemedicine platform shall include assessment, diagnosis, prescriptions and investigations of but not limited to; diarrhoea and vomiting, colds and coughs, urine infections, rashes and allergies, chronic conditions, Malaria, Tuberculosis, Skin and eye problems, depression and anxiety.

No, our service is not currently covered by insurance companies but we are working on it and will be available soon.

Our physicians will give diagnosis and prescribe medications online during your consultation. Your prescription will be on your portal and you can download it anytime.

Every consultation on Bluewave Telemedicine platform is private and provided by a Kenyan licensed physician who must maintain your confidentiality. The information is only accessible to you and your physician and Bluewave or any other party has no access to such information. Also see our Privacy Policy.

Payment is done via Mpesa through the Bluewave paybill 831583. Soon we shall provide credit card payments and insurance facilities.

Bluewave Telemedicine is not intended for medical emergencies. Incase of emergency please visit a hospital.
