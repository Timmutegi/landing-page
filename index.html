FAQs

  • Bluewave Telemedicine uses a technological platform to allow patients via video call to see a Kenyan licensed medical practitioner online upon booking. You as a patient do not need to travel long distances to see a doctor.
  • Bluewave Telemedicine can provide you with a prescription or a diagnosis for your symptoms all online.
  • We can also provide specialist referrals and order tests.
  • Your medical summary and details remain on your portal and you can view them anytime upon logging in.
  • You enroll into the service by registering on the portal, paying the required fees and booking for consultation, a summary of the booking is sent to you via SMS. Once the booking is done, our practitioner accepts the booking and treatment is conducted via video call.

Bluewave Telemedicine is available to all Kenyans of all ages. Children below 18 years should be accompanied by a Parent/Guardian.
